Splitting

Assuming you know that an input can contain at most 2 (or whatever): 
my $d = "goodbye   \"cruel world\"";
say split(/\s+/, $d, 2)[0]; # goodbye
say split(/\s+/, $d, 2)[1]; # "cruel world"
